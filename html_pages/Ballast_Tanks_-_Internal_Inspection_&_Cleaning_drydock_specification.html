REPAIR SPECIFICATION: BALLAST TANKS - INTERNAL INSPECTION & CLEANING

1. DESCRIPTION

This specification covers the internal inspection and cleaning of ballast tanks, including the removal of any debris, sediment, or corrosion that may be present. The work shall include all ballast tanks fitted onboard.

2. SCOPE OF WORK / INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Preparatory Measures:
    • Ensure all ballast tanks are empty and gas-free before commencing work.
    • Obtain necessary permits and approvals before starting the job.
  2. Job Breakdown:
    • Remove any debris, sediment, or corrosion from the ballast tanks.
    • Inspect the internal condition of the ballast tanks and report any defects or damage.
    • Clean and prepare the ballast tanks for future use.
  3. Safety Controls:
    • Ensure all personnel involved in the work are properly trained and equipped with personal protective equipment (PPE).
    • Use proper ventilation and lighting when working inside the ballast tanks.
    • Avoid using open flames or sparks that could ignite flammable vapors.
  4. Standards & Compliance:
    • Comply with all applicable safety and environmental regulations.
    • Follow the manufacturer's instructions and recommendations for the work.
  5. Documentation:
    • Maintain accurate records of the work performed, including before and after photos.
    • Submit a report detailing the work done, any defects or damage found, and recommendations for future maintenance.

3. MATERIAL & SUPPORT

  • Owner Supply:
    • Personal protective equipment (PPE) for personnel involved in the work.
    • Specialized tools and equipment as required for the job.
  • Yard Supply:
    • Cleaning materials and solutions.
    • Ventilation and lighting equipment.
  • Test Equipment Required:
    • Non-destructive testing (NDT) equipment for inspecting the ballast tanks.
    • Measuring and testing tools for verifying the condition of the ballast tanks.
  • Access & Logistics:
    • Provide safe access to the ballast tanks for personnel and equipment.
    • Ensure proper storage and handling of materials and equipment.

4. SERVICE LINES

Service Line Description
Air Compressed air for cleaning and ventilation.
Water Water for cleaning and rinsing the ballast tanks.
Electricity Power for lighting and equipment.
