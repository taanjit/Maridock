REPAIR SPECIFICATION: BALLAST TANK COATING TOUCH-UP / FULL RECOATING

1. DESCRIPTION

This specification covers the touch-up and full recoating of ballast tank coatings, including surface preparation, coating application, and inspection. The work shall include all ballast tanks onboard as part of the vessel's ballast system.
Inventory Identification: MAKE: [MAKE] MODEL: [MODEL] TYPE: [TYPE] OUTPUT / RATING: [OUTPUT / RATING] QUANTITY / NUMBER OF UNITS: [QUANTITY / NUMBER OF UNITS] Location: [LOCATION]

2. SCOPE OF WORK / INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Preparatory Measures:
    • Clean and degrease the ballast tank surfaces
    • Remove any loose or damaged coatings
    • Prepare the surface for coating application
  2. Job Breakdown:
    • Touch-up coating of damaged areas
    • Full recoating of ballast tanks as required
  3. Safety Controls:
    • Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) to be worn at all times
    • Proper ventilation to be ensured during coating application
    • Spark-free tools to be used in hazardous areas
  4. Standards & Compliance:
    • Coating application to meet or exceed manufacturer's recommendations
    • Compliance with relevant classification society and regulatory requirements
  5. Documentation:
    • Records of coating application and inspection to be maintained
    • Reports to be submitted to the Owner and Class Surveyor

3. MATERIAL & SUPPORT

  • Owner Supply:
    • Coating materials and application equipment
  • Yard Supply:
    • Personnel and support services for coating application
  • Test Equipment Required:
    • Coating thickness gauges
    • Surface profile meters
  • Access & Logistics:
    • Proper access to ballast tanks for coating application
    • Logistical support for personnel and equipment
