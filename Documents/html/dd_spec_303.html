SECTION 3: SCOPE OF WORK / INSTRUCTIONS
Preparatory Measures:
  • Obtain necessary permits and safety clearances
  • Isolate system and implement LOTO (Lock Out Tag Out) procedures
  • Prepare work area with adequate lighting, ventilation, and access
  • Brief all personnel on safety requirements and work procedures
  • Arrange transportation to OEM-approved reconditioning facility
  • Coordinate with authorized workshop for scheduling
Job Breakdown:
  1. Removal and Transportation: Disconnect exhaust valve and cylinder head components, carefully remove and transport to approved OEM-authorized reconditioning workshop with proper protection
  2. Exhaust Valve Overhaul: Dismantle valve assembly including spindle, bush, guide, springs, and seat ring, clean all parts and conduct dye penetrant tests, overhaul valve actuator with inspection and calibration
  3. Reconditioning Operations: Machine or grind valve seats to specification, renew casing bush if necessary, replace defective components as required per OEM standards
  4. Cylinder Head Reconditioning: Remove cylinder heads and send for standard OEM reconditioning, clean, inspect, and replace defective components, perform dimensional checks and pressure testing
  5. Testing and Reinstallation: Perform hydraulic pressure test and functional checks, submit test reports and dimensional verification, reinstall with new gaskets and O-rings, conduct final operational testing
Safety Controls:
  • All work to be performed under permit to work system
  • Use appropriate PPE: Safety helmets, safety shoes, coveralls, safety glasses, cut-resistant gloves
  • Ensure proper ventilation and gas-free certification if required
  • Implement fall protection measures where applicable
  • Use certified lifting equipment for heavy component handling
  • Ensure secure transportation and handling of components
  • Hot work permits required for machining and grinding operations
Standards & Compliance:
  • Work to be performed in accordance with OEM manufacturer's instructions
  • Comply with class requirements: [CLASS_REQUIREMENTS]
  • Follow company safety management system procedures
  • Adhere to port state and flag state regulations
Deliverables:
